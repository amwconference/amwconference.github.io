Dr. Yudith Cardinale

Universidad Internacional de Valencia, Spain

Google Scholar ResearchGate
← Back to Information

Research Focus

"High-Performance Computing and Data Semantics in Distributed Environments"

Abstract: Dr. Cardinale's work bridges the gap between massive data processing and semantic interoperability. In the context of modern web foundations, her research explores how distributed architectures can efficiently handle heterogeneous data sources while maintaining consistency and high availability. Her contributions are vital for the evolution of Cloud Computing and IoT ecosystems where data foundations must be both robust and flexible.

Biography

Dr. Yudith Cardinale is a Full Professor at the Universidad Internacional de Valencia (VIU), Spain. She received her Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Université Paris-Sud 11, France. With an extensive academic career, she previously served as a Full Professor at Universidad Simón Bolívar (USB), Venezuela, where she led numerous research initiatives in distributed systems.

Her research interests include Parallel and Distributed Systems, Cloud Computing, Semantic Web, and Web Services. She has authored over 100 scientific publications in top-tier journals and international conferences. Dr. Cardinale is a Senior Member of the IEEE and has been a key collaborator in international research projects focusing on e-Health, digital transition, and scalable data management architectures.
